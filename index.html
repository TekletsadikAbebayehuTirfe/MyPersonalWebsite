About me

My name is Tekletsadik Abebayehu. I am 20 years old and I live in Ethiopia.I am currently learning in Addis Ababa University after joining IT department in 2011 E.C. Now I am a second year student in this campus and this is my personal website.

This website as you can see covers my biography, hobbies, and my contact info.In the biography tab I have tried to jot down my background; where I grew up, where I took my nursury,primary and secondary education, and also a little about the people around me,my friends and family.

When we go ahead and open the 'Hobbies' tab, I'll try to guide to through what I enjoy to do. These activities give me a high amount of relief from any amount of stress I feel at any moment so whenever I feel sad or stressed, these are the activities that immediately turn to. Finally we come to the 'Books' tab. The reason I did not include this books tab in the 'Hobbies' is because I do not only see reading as a recreational activity; I also see it as an essential key for life,like food or water.So having all these things in mind, I have tried to build this personal website by using Dr. Steven Kinear's website as a base(link ) using HTML5 only. So I hope you will like what you will find in my website. Thank you.
